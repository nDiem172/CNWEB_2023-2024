Apple Bavarian Torte ( ★ ★ ★ ★)



A classic European torte baked in a springform pan. Cream cheese, sliced almonds, and apples make this the perfect holiday treat (12 servings).

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup while sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream chesse
  • 1/4 cup while sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 6 apples - peeled cored and sliced
  • 1/3 cup while sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Cream together butter, sugar, vanilla and flour.
  3. Press crust mixture into the first bottom of a 9 inch springfrom pan Set aside.
  4. In a medium bowl blend creams cheese and sugar. Beat in egg and vanilla.Pour cheese mixture over crust.
  5. Toss apples with sugar and cinnamon. Spead apple mixture over all.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) and continue baking for 25 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle almonds over top of tourte. Continue baking until lightly browned. Cool befor removing from pan.
